About

1-Up Jiu Jitsu brings the art of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu to Cleveland, Ohio with Team Chard black belt Paul Bartholet conducting all classes. Classes are conducted in the gi on Tuesdays and Fridays and without the gi on Thursdays. 1-Up Jiu Jitsu is part of Rising Star MMA, and maintains affiliation with Team Chard in Walton Hills, OH. Contact for rates. Small group and private instruction is also available.

Tuesday and Friday are Gi BJJ, Thursday is No Gi. Week day drop-ins are 10 bucks.
